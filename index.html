Health tips

-Stay hydrated

-Eat well

-Get enough sleep

-Exercise

-Take care of your Blood Pressure

Who can donate?

-Age: 18-65 years

-Weight: Above 45kgs

-Interval between two donations must be 3-4 months

-Donor must be healthy

-Hemoglobin levels must be above 12.5gm/dl

Why to donate?

-It is beneficial for human health

-Helps the people who are in need

-Reduces cancer risk

-Boosts the production RBC

-Replenishes blood
